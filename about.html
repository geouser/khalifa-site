About Khalifa Al Maftool Advocates & Legal Consultants

Khalifa Al Maftool Advocates & Legal Consultants office was established in 1978 as a one of the legal institutions in the United Arab Emirates, and known after its high-end solutions either in legal or consultancy services.

Our success is based on unprecedented accumulated experience in dealing with legal issues, negotiation, arbitration and litigation available to our customers to answer all inquiries and provide high-quality services in order to meet client’s interests and building a strong and a long-term relationship.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to achieve objectives while adhering the core values of honour, integrity, impartiality, objectivity, honesty and credibility.

We aimed at the rule of law and achieving the truth and fair justice with all honesty and credibility.

OUR PURPOSE

To be in the top ranks in this great profession and provide our services to our members in the best way as possible as we can, other than this shall not be accepted because we are so keen for the legal interests of our Office costumers.

OUR MISSION

We strive hard to defend the honor of our profession by adopting the highest degree of professionalism, craftsmanship and the preservation of our much valued client secrets. We also communicate the latest developments regarding their issues and help them to choose the best available options. Our value added services make us the top choice for everyone looking for legal services.
